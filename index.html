Across universities in Uganda, many students struggle with food insecurity while pursuing higher education. Meals are skipped quietly. Hunger is hidden behind resilience. Yet learning, health, and wellbeing are deeply affected. Learn And Lunch exists to make campus hunger visible, understood, and addressed through ethical, student led systems.
